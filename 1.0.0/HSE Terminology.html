HSE Terminology

Abstract

A controlled vocabulary for the Health Service Executive public health terminology including code schemes for data catalogues and health indicators. This terminology provides standardized terms and classifications for health data management, supporting interoperability and consistent data cataloguing across the Irish health system.

Abbreviations

  • Definition: DEF
  • Topmost Term of the hierarchy: TT
  • Broader Term: BT
  • Narrower Term: NT
